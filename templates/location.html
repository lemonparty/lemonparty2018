Location

Our wedding will be in Katherine’s hometown, Duluth, Minnesota.

The ceremony and reception will be at Lake Breeze Motel Resort, located 15 minutes up the shore from downtown Duluth at 9000 Congdon Boulevard, Duluth, MN 55804. Lake Breeze has a heated outdoor pool, so bring a bathing suit!

Around this time in Duluth, the average temperature range for a day is 56º-76º. So expect a lovely day, and the need for a sweater or jacket in the evening. Unless it rains, then all bets are off.

To get to Duluth, you can fly into Duluth International Airport (DLH). Once there, you can rent a car at the airport, or use a ride-sharing service like Lyft. If you prefer longer journeys, you could fly to Minneapolis (MSP) and either drive, or take the Skyline Shuttle to Duluth.
